# Deep Track Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 22, 2026  
**Last Updated:** May 22, 2026  

At Deep Track ("we", "our", or "us"), we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled when you download, install, and use the Deep Track desktop application ("Software").

---

### 1. Privacy-First Architecture
Deep Track is designed with a local-first architecture. This means:
*   **Local Storage:** Your productivity records, task lists, study journals, and screen-time histories are stored **strictly on your local machine** (in your local application data directory).
*   **No PII Collection:** We do **not** collect, sell, or trade any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as your name, email address, password, or physical location.
*   **No Keystroke or Screen Logging:** The Software does **not** record your keystrokes, capture screenshots, or monitor the contents of what you write in your notes.

### 2. Anonymous Telemetry and Analytics (Opt-Out Available)
To help us debug errors, verify release stability, and improve features, the Software includes an optional, anonymous usage telemetry system (powered by PostHog). 

#### A. What we capture:
If telemetry is enabled, the app transmits anonymous events including:
*   **Application Boot Heartbeats:** A single ping sent once per day to help count active users.
*   **Feature Actions:** Interactions such as starting a focus session or switching tabs.
*   **System Context:** Your Operating System type (Windows/macOS), the version of Deep Track you are running, and an automatically generated, randomized anonymous user identifier (e.g., `dt_xyz123`).

#### B. What we do NOT capture:
We do not link these analytics to any personal identity, nor do we track external browser histories, files, or background applications.

#### C. Control Your Data (Opt-Out):
You are in complete control of your telemetry. You can turn this off at any time:
1. Open Deep Track and click on the **Settings** icon.
2. In the **General** section, uncheck the **"Share Usage Telemetry"** option.
3. Click **Save Settings**. 
Once unchecked, no further telemetry events will be sent.

### 3. User-Initiated Support & Feedback
If you choose to submit feedback via the built-in Feedback Hub:
*   **Voluntary Text:** We collect the text explanation, satisfaction rating, and standard system diagnostics (App version, OS version, and session statistics) you submit.
*   **Secure Delivery:** This data is sent securely to our developer channels to help us resolve bugs.
*   **Alternative Clipboard Submission:** You can also copy these logs directly to your clipboard to submit them manually.

### 4. Third-Party Services
We use **PostHog** to ingest and process anonymous analytics. All communications are sent securely over HTTPS. PostHog does not share or commercialize this data.

### 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will indicate the date of the latest update at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
